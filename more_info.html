We are hockey fans asking the National Hockey League to let fans buy authentic, on-ice, Made in Canada jerseys at retail.

Where can I get more info?

Stop Calling Adidas NHL Jerseys Authentic - Teal Town USA's AJ Strong outlines, with clear photos, the recent history of NHL jerseys, and the differences between Indonesia and Canadian made jerseys.

Adidas MiC v. Adidas Retail v. Fanatics Breakaway - Liberty Bell Jerseys has compiled a collection of high-resolution photos detailing the differences between Adidas Made in Canada jerseys, Adidas Made in Indonesia jerseys, and Fanatics Breakaway (Made in Vietnam) jerseys.

r/hockeyjerseys - Reddit's largest hockey jersey community. A great place to seek out more information and ask questions.
